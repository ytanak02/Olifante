OLIFANTÉ

Your Zen Starts Here

OLIFANTé is a small sustainable tea company that helps support the fight to end elephant poaching and abuse while sharing the culture of tea with people around the world. We are focused on a sustainable tea exchange and our mission is to not only offer the best tea around but to also gather animal loving customers and raise awareness of endangered animals. Our tea is specially bought directly from carefully chosen tea farmers who support this fight. Come join our tea party to save a life!
